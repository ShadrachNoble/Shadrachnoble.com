About

Born in Kentucky, raised in San Antonio, and now living near the waters of the South Sound in University Place, Washington, I am a husband, a father of three, and a thinker drawn to the edges of experience. My work explores the liminal, the unexplained, and the deeply symbolic intersections between science, mysticism, and art.
