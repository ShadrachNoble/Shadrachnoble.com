Shadrach Noble

Art, Theory, and the Echoes of Elsewhere

Shadrach Noble is a Kentucky-born author, artist, and independent theorist living in the South Sound region of Washington State. A husband and father of three, his work bridges metaphysics, visual symbolism, and the unexplained. He is the originator of Dimensional Residue Theory, a novel framework for interpreting phenomena that defy simple categorization. His writing, art, and research seek not to solve mystery, but to preserve it with reverence and clarity.
