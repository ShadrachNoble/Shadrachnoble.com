Shadrach Noble

Art, Theory, and the Echoes of Elsewhere

About

Shadrach Noble is a Kentucky-born writer, theorist, and filmmaker. He lives with his wife and three children in a small community near the southern shores of Puget Sound. His work explores the interplay between reality and the unknown—often drawing on themes of contact, memory, and the metaphysical.

Work

Imprints from Elsewhere (Poetic Version)

A lyrical introduction to Dimensional Residue Theory, weaving sacred geometry, ontological shock, and speculative metaphysics.

Imprints from Elsewhere (Formal Version)

A structured academic presentation of the theory, referencing thinkers like Jacques Vallée, John Keel, and Paul Laffoley.
